# PK Basics: AUC

Area under the curve (AUC) over 24 hours captures total exposure. For vancomycin, targeting an AUC24/MIC of 400–600 (assuming MIC=1) balances efficacy and nephrotoxicity risk per 2020 ASHP/IDSA guidance.

## Why AUC instead of trough-only?

Troughs correlate imperfectly with AUC and can lead to overexposure. Bayesian methods can estimate AUC from fewer levels by leveraging population priors.

## MIC assumptions

When MIC is unknown, many institutions assume MIC=1 mg/L for the AUC24/MIC target. If true MIC differs, interpret the ratio accordingly.
