# AUC Targets

Target AUC24/MIC 400–600 assuming MIC=1 mg/L based on 2020 ASHP/IDSA consensus. This range seeks efficacy while limiting nephrotoxicity.

- If the MIC is higher than 1 mg/L, achieving the ratio may require alternative strategies.
- Always interpret in clinical context and monitor renal function.
