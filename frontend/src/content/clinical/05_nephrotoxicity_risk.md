# Nephrotoxicity Risk

Risk factors may include high AUC exposure, prolonged therapy, concurrent nephrotoxins, critical illness, and unstable renal function. Monitor serum creatinine and urine output.

- Consider lower initial exposure in high-risk patients.
- Reassess frequently with changing renal function.
