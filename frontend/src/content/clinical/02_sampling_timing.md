# Sampling & Timing

## One level (Bayesian)

- Preferred single level is 1–2 hours after infusion ends (post-distribution).
- A trough alone is generally insufficient to target AUC precisely.

## Two levels (first-order PK)

- Post-distribution "peak" 1–2 h after infusion end.
- Trough ≤1 h prior to the next dose (i.e., near τ).

## Documentation tips

- Record exact times relative to infusion start and end.
- Note infusion duration (≥60 min for 1 g; longer for larger doses).
