# Welcome to the Tutorial

This tutorial shows how to use the app for AUC-guided vancomycin dosing and explains core pharmacokinetic concepts.

## What you'll learn

- How dose (mg), interval (τ), and infusion time shape the concentration–time curve.
- Why AUC24/MIC 400–600 is recommended when MIC=1 (2020 ASHP/IDSA).
- How to time levels: one-level Bayesian vs two-level first-order PK.

## Quick tips

- Prefer a single post-distribution level 1–2 h after infusion ends for Bayesian updates.
- If using two-level first-order PK, collect a post-distribution "peak" (1–2 h after infusion end) and a trough ≤1 h pre-dose.
- Infuse ≥60 min for 1 g; consider 90–120 min for 1.5–2 g.
